About PEP: Founded On Passion

Primus E Pluribus was born out of an obsession with ideas and the ways in which spoken languages give those voice. With nearly ten years of overseas experience in the service of the U.S. Federal government as a tenured Foreign Service Officer (in layman's terms: a diplomat) with the Department of State, two university degrees in language and area studies, and knowledge of ten languages, there is simply no one more qualified to help you harness the power of language in your favor, whatever your bottom line might be.

The Methodology makes
The DIFFERENCE

Traditional language learning methodologies don't take into account two very important principles in second language learning: active participation and immersive instruction. Using the exact same methodology with which the State Department brings its diplomats from zero language ability to full fluency in as little as six months* combined with techniques learned from trial and error in learning ten language, PEP adapts its instruction to the needs of the organization or individual and provides an active and immersive environment in which language skills are expanded and strengthened.

* Results vary by language and are based on full time 40 hour a week study.
